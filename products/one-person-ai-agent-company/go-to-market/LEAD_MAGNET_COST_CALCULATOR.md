# Lead Magnet: AI Agent Cost Calculator

## Purpose

Show visitors how much they save by replacing their first hire with AI agents.

## Inputs

- Current monthly salary / contractor cost
- Hours spent on admin, sales, support
- Desired tasks to automate

## Output

- Annual cost: human hire vs. AI agent company
- Breakdown by agent role
- Suggested 3-agent starter setup

## CTA

"Get the full 7-agent template — $149"

## Hosting

Single HTML file. Can be deployed via GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Cloudflare Pages.
