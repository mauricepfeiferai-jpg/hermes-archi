# Product Factory Agent: Retro

## Role

After every factory run, log lessons to the 100x loop.

## Questions

1. What worked in this run?
2. What was missing?
3. What should the factory do differently next time?
4. Which template needs an update?
5. What new agent role is needed?

## Output

- Append to `FACTORY_RUN_RETROSPECTIVE.md`
- Suggest improvement patches to agents/ or templates/
