## Core Decision

Inspired by Marc Lou: stop overbuilding, start shipping.

From this point forward, every AI Empire asset follows the same rhythm:

1. **Build small** — minimum viable product/skill/template/post
2. **Ship fast** — commit, push, publish
3. **Measure** — signups, sales, engagement
4. **Iterate** — double down on winners, kill losers fast
5. **Document** — every result becomes a Gold Nugget

## Trial & Error Rules

- Every experiment gets a 7-day test.
- If no signal after 7 days → kill or pivot.
- If positive signal after 3 days → double down.
- No project lives without a next proof step.
- Every failure gets a Gold Nugget + 100x loop improvement.

## Monetization First

Every asset must answer:
- How does someone pay for this?
- What is the one-time price?
- Where is the buy button?

No free plan. Hard paywall. Popcorn pricing.
